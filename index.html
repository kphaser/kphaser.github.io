Kevin Wong is a graduate student in predictive analytics at Northwestern University. His interests include machine learning and data visualization at scale. He was recently a Quantitative Research Analyst for a financial startup and formerly an accountant; so numbers and details are in his blood. When he’s not doing data science, he is having conversations about the NBA or enjoying board games with his wife and friends!
