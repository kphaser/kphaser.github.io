First Post using Hugo

Sun, Dec 4, 2016 Hugo

I’ve used many different frameworks and platforms for blogging from Blogspot to Wordpress to Ghost to Jekyll. R Markdown is awesome, but it’s never been easy to share R Markdown documents on any of those platforms. Hugo is another blogging framework, which I’ve heard great things about. It compiles very quickly and has little dependencies to worry about compared to Jekyll. I’m going to give it a try and hopefully it will play nicely with R Markdown.

MSPA Review and the Data Science Path

Tue, Sep 13, 2016 MSPA

One of the most interesting things about data science is the diversity of practitioners’ backgrounds. I’ve met people who are very science-y (Physics, Astronomy) to very technical (Computer Science, Mathematics) to very business-y (Marketing, IT). There is no definitive path to data science aside from getting a MS or PhD in Statistics or Computer Science since data science involves statistics and software development skills. It’s the most interesting field currently with lots of freedom to explore, to build, to learn, and to shape the future.
